Farm-to-table

Root partners with thousands of farmers all across America to bring our food to life. The root of our taste comes from our organic, fresh, and non GMO produce that is shipped directly from the source. We have manually assessed the quality of all of our farms to ensure the highest caliber of freshness for our products. We believe that to have the best quality you have to go to the root of where it begins. Our Root employees make sure that the produce is up to our quality standards before they are shipped straight to our establishments where they are prepared in location.

Sustainability

We utilize solar panels to ensure sustainable energy so that no matter what happens we can deliver our customers the freshest taste that we possibly can.
To ensure that no food is gone to waste we use a method of cold composting to make sure that no matter what we are giving back to the environment in any way that we possibly can. Then our compost is made into fertilizer that we redistribute to our farmers to allow for better sustainability within our management.

All of Root is made using sustainable resources, from bioplastics that are used to make our highly durable and reliable bowls, to recycled plastic fibers to make our silverware prepared for anything

Our Process

1. Ingredient Sourcing.

Root sources our ingredients from our inspected farmers from all across the country to ensure that our ingredients are fresh as well as GMO free and naturally grown.

Our Process

2. Receiving and Storing Ingredients.

After receiving all of our ingredients we inspect our supply to further ensure freshness and quality, during this process we also mark down each and every one of our ingredients in a checklist so that we know exactly how much inventory we have and how we can plan the week conscious of our supply, as well as mark each ingredient with proper use by dates. After, we immediately store each of our ingredients in their respective places to avoid any food waste and to ensure the maximum quality that we can provide for our customers. Another approach that we at Root use is a first in first out approach so that the first ingredients that we obtain are the first ingredients that we use to make certain that we avoid waste.

Our Process

3. Station Preparation.

At Root we have specific areas for chopping each ingredient, cooking, and mixing, this allows for us to be considerate about health risks such as allergies that our customers may have and avoid cross contamination. To further establish a nice clean kitchen environment each of our cooking utensils, tools and surfaces are sanitized to further avoid cross contamination and bacteria. We realize that a big part of customer service is to make sure that customers have an expedient experience here at Root so to help with that we work to make sure that our serving sizes are prepped so that no time is wasted establishing what could have already been established.

Our Process

4. Cooking and Assembly Process.

To minimize the chance of us getting something wrong within your order and so that we dont simply rely off of memory, Root holds all of our recipes and clear steps for each dish in a master list which our employees are trained to use to make sure that each and every order is made with precision and accuracy.

Our Process

5. Clean Up and Reset.

At the end of each day we make sure all of our cooking utensil get washed, sanitized and finally stored so that we end each day exactly where we started, this allows us to make sure there's nothing that we shouldn't expect and that we're prepared to deliver you the freshest experience that we possibly can.
