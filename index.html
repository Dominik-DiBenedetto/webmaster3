Farm-to-table

Root partners with thousands of farmers all across America to bring our food to life. The root of our taste comes from our organic, fresh, and non GMO produce that is shipped directly from the source. We have manually assessed the quality of all of our farms to ensure the highest caliber of freshness for our products. We believe that to have the best quality you have to go to the root of where it begins. Our Root employees make sure that the produce is up to our quality standards before they are shipped straight to our establishments where they are prepared in location.

Sustainability

We utilize solar panels to ensure sustainable energy so that no matter what happens we can deliver our customers the freshest taste that we possibly can.
